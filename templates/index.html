Let's be honest...searching for the next bottle of whisky can be a daunting task. Whether you are standing in front of hundreds of bottles in a package store, or thumbing through a 50+ page menu at a local whisky bar, sometimes the multitude of options just makes choosing that much more difficult. To address this problem, this site was created with the hopes of helping users find new and exciting whiskies through the use of exploratory and visual data analysis tools. Using these tools, we can factor in a variety of metrics such as: country of origin, region, age, cask type, grain or malt ratio, flavor profiles, characteristics, price, proof and more.
